Rafting Site Plan

Miriam Call

Overview

Purpose

I am advertizing my rafting business online so those who want to go rafting will know where to get their gear and a guide. This website is designed to make it easy and convienent for my consumers to find the things they are looking for when going rafting.

Audience

"Who are the target customers?" My customers are those seeking to go rafting. It will typically be teens to young adults that will be using my website. "What do they expect to find?" They expect to find information about rafting and a place to purchase or rent rafting equiptment. Those items are the things they will find on my website. "How will my audience usually access my site?" I feel my audience will access my site typically on a mobile device or laptop. They are of a younger generation and used to having everything at their fingertips. This means they will access the information in a way that is easiest for them, their electronic that is on them already.
